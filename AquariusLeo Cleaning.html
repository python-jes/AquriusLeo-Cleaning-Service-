About Us

We Are AquariusLeo Cleaning

A trusted name in professional cleaning services, dedicated to making every space spotless and every client satisfied.

Founded over a decade ago, AquariusLeo Cleaning was built on a simple belief — everyone deserves a clean, healthy, and comfortable environment. What started as a small residential cleaning team has grown into a full-service cleaning company serving hundreds of happy clients across the region.

Our team of background-checked, trained professionals brings skill, care, and attention to every job. We use eco-friendly products that are safe for your family, pets, and the planet — because we believe clean shouldn't come at a cost to your health.

Whether it's your home, office, or a post-construction site, we show up on time, work with precision, and don't leave until you're completely satisfied.
